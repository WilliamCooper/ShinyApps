Use the “Degree of Polynomial” entry window to specify the degree of the polynomial to fit to the calibration data. The fit is obtained in the form x=f(M), as needed to use the calibration to obtain estimates of a measurand from the output of this sensor. The red line displays the results from the fit, and the text box below the plot summarizes the results. Note especially the “Residual standard deviation” that shows how well the curve fits the data. This is the error in x and so will be a typical error arising from using this calibration.

Use the “M=f(x)” checkbox to exchange the independent and dependent variables in the regression; i.e., to regress M as a function of x. See the discussion under “our solution” for reasons that this is not a good approach.
