Checking PSXC and QCXC: Recommendations

  1. Start with the "Review" tab; see plots 9--12. Use "brush" to zoom to problems. Look for general consistency with past projects.

  2. Use the "Past Projects" tab and the secondary tabs "PSXC" and "QCXC". These provide comparisons to PS_A and QC_A, which should be consistent with past projects.

  3. Also under "Past Projects" -> "PSXC", see the "hydrostatic-eq check" tab; measurements should be consistent to within a few hPa.
