Checking WSC/WDC (horizontal wind): Recommendations

  1. Start with the "Review" tab plots 13--16. Plot 15 should show a reasonable Schuler oscillation in the difference, remaining approximately within the dotted red lines. Plot 16 should show only a minor Schuler oscillation for DVEWG/DVNSG (green lines), the difference between the result of the complementary-filter and the original GPS measurement. Use "brush" to zoom to problems. Look for general consistency with past projects. Also check both panels in plot 17, where (outside of turns) AKRD should be in approximate agreement with AOAREF and SSRD should be in reasonable agreement with SSREF.

  2. Use the "Past Projects" -> "Maneuvers" tab to check any speed-run, pitch, circle, reverse-heading, or yaw maneuvers that are present.

  3. As for all variables, use the "Known Problems" -> "out-of-range, frozen, spike" tab to check for problems with the basic (i.e., raw, not derived) measurements entering the wind calculation. These include the measurements from the INS and the pressure differences from the radome or pitot tube.
