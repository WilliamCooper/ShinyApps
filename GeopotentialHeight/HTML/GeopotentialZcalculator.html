Using the Geopotential Height Calculator

The latitude entered in the top left entry slot (in degrees) will be used to construct the plot on the right, showing the difference between the geometric height and geopotential height as a function of geopotential height. When the mouse-controlled cursor is placed over the plotted line, corresponding values of geopotential and geometric height will be displayed along with the difference.

To get refined conversions, use the entry slot in the left panel for "height" and select the radio-button corresponding to the conversion desired, either from geometric to geopotential height or the reverse. The appropriate correspondence will be shown at the bottom of the shaded portion of the left panel.

Suggested exercise: Explore how the conversion depends on latitude, and suggest the reason for this variation.
