The interactive graph in the right panel allows you to explore how the temperature of a dry air parcel, or a moist air parcel in the absence of condensation, will change as the pressure changes. The results for low pressure or low temperature may be unrealistic because of condensation that will occur when the parcel reaches 100% relative humidity, so at that point the displayed lines step when moist air is selected. This point is referred to as the lifted condensation level or LCL.
