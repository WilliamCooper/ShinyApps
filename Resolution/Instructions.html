Instructions:

Use the checkboxes to select the desired level of confidence at which you would like to conclude, on the basis of measurements of each measurand, that the measurands are indeed different. The slider below then sets the assumed difference between two measurands. Adjust this value until P2 matches the selected confidence level. This setting of the slider specifies the resolution of the instrument, the separation between measurands at which you can use measurements of the two measurands to conclude with the specified confidence that the measurands are different.
